1. Overview

WaterSonic is a utility app for water ejection, sleep sound playback, and environmental noise reference tools. The app is intentionally designed to operate without user registration, account login, or a personal profile system.

We do not build a consumer profile around your identity. In normal use, WaterSonic is intended to function as a local, device-based utility.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

We do not require or intentionally collect personal user information to let you use the app. In particular, we do not require account creation, and we do not maintain a user login system in the current version.

No account login No user profile No identity-based tracking No personal data sales
  • No name or email is required to use the core app features.
  • No contact list, photos, camera data, or precise location data is requested for normal app use.
  • No social graph or account-based usage history is built by the app itself.

3. Data Processing Model

WaterSonic is designed to process as little information as possible. Most core functionality runs on-device. Where audio playback or system permissions are involved, processing is limited to the immediate function you are using.

  • Water Eject: playback is generated locally on your device.
  • Sleep Modes: sounds and timers are handled on-device for the session you start.
  • Noise Meter: the app may request microphone access only if a feature requires live measurement, and the information is used for that session only.

We do not intentionally retain a historical personal dataset for marketing or behavioral profiling.

4. Third-Party and Platform Services

WaterSonic may rely on Apple platform services for distribution, purchases, subscription management, crash reporting, or system permissions. Those services are governed by Apple’s own terms and policies.

If you contact support by email, we will receive the information you choose to send, such as your email address and the contents of your message. We use that information only to respond and provide support.

5. How We Use Limited Information

If any limited information is processed, it is used only to:

  • deliver the app’s core functions;
  • support app stability and debugging;
  • respond to support requests;
  • process purchases or entitlements where applicable;
  • meet legal, security, and compliance obligations.

6. Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell personal information. We do not share user profiles because the app does not use an account-based user profile system.

Limited technical data may be processed by infrastructure providers as needed to operate app distribution, purchases, or support systems. Those providers are expected to handle data under their own privacy terms and applicable law.

7. Retention

Because the app does not require account login and is designed not to collect personal user information, we generally do not maintain persistent personal records for ordinary app use.

If you send us support email, we may retain the correspondence for as long as reasonably necessary to respond, maintain records, resolve disputes, or comply with legal obligations.
