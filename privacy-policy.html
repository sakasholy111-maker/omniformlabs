1.Summary

WaterSonic is a utility application that helps users push moisture from a device speaker, view a reference reading of environmental sound, and play ambient audio for sleep. WaterSonic does not require account registration. We do not sell personal information, we do not run advertising, and we do not use the data we receive to track you across other apps or websites.

This policy is written to be consistent with the App Privacy "nutrition label" we file in Apple App Store Connect for this application. Where this policy and the App Store label disagree, the more protective interpretation should apply, and we will correct the inconsistency.

2.Microphone Input

When you open the Noise Meter feature and start a measurement, WaterSonic asks for microphone permission. The audio stream is read on your device for the active session and used only to compute a live decibel reference value.

Audio is not recorded, not transmitted, and not stored by us, by Apple, or by any third-party processor. If you deny microphone permission, the Noise Meter will not function, but every other feature continues to work.

3.Purchases

Subscriptions and purchases are processed by Apple through StoreKit and the App Store. To unlock and maintain Premium access, we use a third-party subscription infrastructure called Adapty (Adapty Pte. Ltd., https://adapty.io).

Adapty receives the following information about your transactions:

  • the product identifier you purchased, for example com.omniformlabs.watersonic.monthly;
  • subscription state (active, in trial, expired, refunded);
  • transaction timestamps used for renewal and entitlement checks;
  • renewal events relayed by Apple's App Store Server Notifications.

Adapty does not receive your Apple ID, your name, your email address, or your payment card information.

4.Identifiers

Adapty assigns each application installation a random, anonymous Adapty Profile ID. The Profile ID is used to know which device has Premium access and to restore purchases when you reinstall.

Adapty may also use Apple's per-vendor identifier (IDFV) to recognize the same device across launches of WaterSonic. The IDFV is not the advertising identifier (IDFA); it is not shared across apps from different vendors and cannot be used by other developers' apps to identify you.

5.Usage Data

Adapty collects events about Premium-related interactions in the application, such as:

  • when a paywall is shown;
  • which products are visible and which one is selected;
  • whether the purchase flow is started, completed, or cancelled;
  • restoration attempts.

These events are used to operate, debug, and analyze the subscription experience — for example, to confirm that the paywall is rendering correctly and that purchases are converting. They are not used for advertising and are not shared with advertising networks.

6.Diagnostics

Crash reports and basic performance signals from the application may be processed by Apple (through standard iOS crash reporting) and by Adapty (in connection with the SDK). Diagnostics are used to debug, stabilize, and improve the application. They do not contain the contents of any audio recording, message, or document.

7.Support Communications

If you contact us by email, we receive the information you choose to include in your message — typically your email address, the device model, the application version, and the issue you are reporting. We use this information only to respond to you and to maintain a record of the request.

8.Tracking

WaterSonic does not use the data above to track you across applications or websites operated by other companies. WaterSonic does not display advertising and does not use the IDFA. We do not invoke App Tracking Transparency and we will not ask you to grant tracking permission, because we do not need it.

9.Connectivity and Network Use

WaterSonic is designed to work offline for its core features. Specifically:

  • Water Eject, Sleep, and Noise Meter run fully on-device and do not require internet access. They use audio synthesis and the microphone locally, with no outbound network traffic.
  • The paywall, purchase, and restore-purchase flows require network access. Without connectivity, these screens display a loading state and then a retry option; the offline state never blocks free features.
  • Premium entitlement is cached locally. If you have already subscribed, your Premium status remains available offline.

All outbound traffic uses HTTPS over TLS 1.2 or higher. The destinations are limited to Apple's App Store endpoints and Adapty's servers (api.adapty.io and related subdomains). The application does not perform background uploads of user content, audio, or contact data, and it does not access the local network, Bonjour, AirPlay discovery, or Wi-Fi SSID information.

  • Apple-managed transaction records are retained per Apple's policies.

  • Access and deletion: if applicable law (including the EU and UK GDPR, the California CCPA/CPRA, and similar regimes) gives you a right to access, correct, delete, or restrict processing of personal data we hold about you, email sakasholy111@gmail.com with the subject line Privacy Request. Because the application does not require an account, we may need to ask for the Adapty Profile ID or other context to identify your records.
