2.Service Description

WaterSonic is a focused audio utility application for iPhone. It provides:

  • Water Eject — playback modes that vibrate the speaker enclosure with tuned low-frequency audio in order to help move moisture out of the speaker grille.
  • Noise Meter — a live, on-device decibel reference reading driven by your microphone.
  • Sleep — five ambient audio modes with timer-based playback designed to mask distractions.

WaterSonic is intended for general consumer convenience. Features may be added, removed, or changed in future versions. We may also add localized content, additional product tiers, or further utility features, and we may discontinue features that are no longer aligned with the application's purpose.
